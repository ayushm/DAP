Items Catalog

 

1.      General Usage & Navigation

a)      Navigate through Items in the table by clicking, using the up and down arrow keys on your keyboard, fields below get populated with the current selected row in the table

b)      Use the functions on the right to perform them on the selected item.

c)      Drag the column headers on the table to change the viewing order based on your personal preference.

 

2.      Add New Item

a)      From the HOME screen, click CATALOG.

b)      In the bottom right corner of the catalog window, click on NEW.

c)      Enter the appropriate information in all the text fields.

d)      When this has been completed, click SAVE (or CANCEL to cancel creation).

 

3.       Edit Item Information

a)      From the HOME screen, click on CATALOG.

b)      Select your desired item in the table or search for it in the search bar above and click on the correct item in the new table.

c)      Once your item is selected, in the bottom right corner of the catalog window, click on EDIT.

d)      Change the desired item information in the correct text field.

e)      When this has been completed, click SAVE. (or CANCEL to clear all changes).

 

4.      Delete Item

a)      From the HOME screen, click on CATALOG.

b)      Select your desired item in the table or search for it in the search bar above and click on the correct item in the new table.

c)      Once your item is selected, in the bottom right corner of the catalog window, click on REMOVE.

d)      Confirm deletion in the dialog box.

 

5.      Search For An Item

a)      At the top of the panel, select the desired search criteria from the drop-down menu.

b)      Enter the text to be searched in the text field next to the drop-down menu.

c)      Press ENTER on your keyboard or click the search button with the search icon on the right of the text field.

d)      You will now see the search results on the table or a dialog box if there were no matches for your search

e)      From here you can Edit, Remove, and make a New item(see instructions above)

f)       To go back to the full catalog, click the FULL CATALOG button.

g)      Your previous searched-text is preserved in the search text field above.
