Welcome to Library Management System!

Library Management System is an application designed to help schools and libraries to complete the various library tasks easily and efficiently. Library Management System keeps track of all the Items and Patrons and provides Catalog search facility along with easy checkout and return functionality.

 

 

A.   Search the Catalog

1)      Select the search criteria from the drop-down menu at the top of the screen

2)      Enter search text in the text field

3)      Press ENTER on your keyboard or click the button with the search icon on the right

4)      A new panel will be displayed showing the search results in a table

5)      From this screen click HOME, to return to the main screen

 

 

 

 

 

B.   Navigate to Various Screens

1)      Click on CATALOG with the icon of a book to maintain Items (Books, CDs, DVDs etc.)

2)      Click on PATRONS with the icon of two business-men figures to maintain Patron information

3)      Click on TYPES with the icon of two CD’s to maintain Item Types

4)      Click on REPORTS with the icon of two documents to navigate to the Reports Screen

5)      Click on Checkout/Return with the icon of shopping cart to Checkout and Return Items

6)      To return back to the main screen, click HOME on the subsequent screen.

 

 

F.   File Menu

1)      Write All Text Files (CTRL + S)

·         Click on FILE in the menu bar at the top of the application (or press ALT + F on your keyboard)

·         Click on SAVE CHANGES

 

2)      Take Backup of data files

·         Click on FILE in the menu bar at the top of the application (or press ALT + F on your keyboard

·         Click on GENERATE BACKUP FILES

3)      Restore from a previous Backup

·         Click on FILE in the menu bar at the top of the application (or press ALT + F on your keyboard)

·         Click on RESTORE FROM BACKUP

 

 

 

H.   Application Help and About

1.      Accessing Help Topics

·         Click on HELP in the menu bar at the top of the application (or press ALT + H on your keyboard)

·         Click on HELP (or press F1 on your keyboard)

2.      About– Getting information about the application

·         Click on HELP in the menu bar at the top of the application (or press ALT + H on your keyboard)

·         Click on ABOUT (or press ALT + A on your keyboard)

 

 

 

 

H.   Application User Preference Setting

1.      Accessing Help Topics

·         Click on Options in the menu bar at the top of the application

·         Switch on or off the Instant Search feature on Catalog and Patron’s search windows.
